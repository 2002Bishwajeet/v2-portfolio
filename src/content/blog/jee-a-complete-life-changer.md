As the title suggests, this exam has changed a lot of student's lives. So for those readers who don’t know what JEE is, it stands for Joint Entrance Examination which is necessary to give to gain admission to top government engineering colleges in India. It further divides into two parts JEE Main and JEE Advanced: those who qualify for JEE mains give JEE Advanced to gain admission to IIT(Indian Institute of Technology) , the reputed Tier-1 College of India.

Anyways Back to My Story:

**I had a great passion for computers and other technical stuff and the only way to study them is to do Engineering. And For Engineering, you need to obtain Physics , Chemistry , Mathematics as the main subject in XI grade and you need to give JEE Mains thus to get qualified to get into better Engineering Colleges. At that time — being in a better college means successful life and guaranteed placements(though it’s a myth as I know the reality now).**

So when I was in X grade , I enrolled into reputed coaching institute for JEE prep. Little I know that it would have a drastic effect in my life. Those 3 years studying there had completely changed my perspective of viewing the world. I had never been exposed in a competitive environment before. Weekly Test, Doubt Classes — there was just too much competition . Thus, I couldn’t cope with the current competition and I couldn’t get into a Top Batch (also called Sankalp Batches). 

I was sad as I couldn’t live up to the expectation of my parents but I made improvements , I was able to get into a decent batch at the end of my X grade where they start Teaching XI grade topics early . It was a achievement , at least for me as I was getting a Boost among others. I was very happy as I was able to know that I can crack IITJEE . I have the ability to do it . This made me more motivated and I have decided to not get behind this time. I attended regular doubt session classes, increased the time of my self study, gave test and had a good rank in them.

Then Something Happened

  

 But that too short lived, I was slow in grasping the concepts and the curriculum was a little too much for me at the start. The moment I regained my pace, I lost the chance of going into Sankalp Batch in XI grade(I couldn’t perform well in my test). So, I was still stuck in Normal Batches(The quality isn’t reduced but the frequency of classes are less in normal batch than the Sankalp batches).

**In this journey I made a lot of Friends(some of them are still in contact), I had lot’s of incidents — some of them were memorable some were not.**
